Details

A more in depth look at what each service package provides.

Day of Coordination

For the couple that has planned their perfect day and just want some help making sure everything goes off without a hitch on the big day!

This Includes:
♥ Timeline Creation
♥ Ceremony Rehearsal
♥ Wedding Day Coordination of Vendors
♥ Management of the Ceremony
♥ Management of the Reception

Starting at $1,200

Partial Planning

For the couple that wants help planning their big day and fully realizing their vision. I can help source Vendors and make suggestions. Once secured, I will be responsible for communicating with Vendors up to and throughout the wedding day. Of course, I will also be in charge of day-of coordination and management for you!

This Includes:
♥ Communicating with Vendors
♥ Executing vision
♥ Timeline Creation
♥ Ceremony Rehearsal
♥ Coordination of Vendors
♥ Management of Ceremony
♥ Management of Reception

Starting at $2,200

Full Service Planning

For the couple that wants a guide helping them plan their wedding from start to finish. We will have a detailed consultation meeting to create the vision of your dream wedding. Then I will assist with sourcing and booking Vendors. I will be the main point of contact for all Vendors throughout the whole process from planning all the way though the wedding day. All day of services will also be performed.

This Includes:
♥ Creating vision
♥ Communicating with Vendors
♥ Executing vision
♥ Timeline Creation
♥ Ceremony Rehearsal
♥ Coordination of Vendors
♥ Management of Ceremony
♥ Management of Reception

Starting at $3,250
